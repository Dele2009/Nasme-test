Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is a Unit?
A Unit is a department or area within your organization, such as IT, real estate, or sports.

2. Can I add multiple units?
Yes, you can add as many units as necessary to structure your organization efficiently.

3. How can I manage these units later?
You can manage units through the admin panel, where you can edit or delete them as needed.
